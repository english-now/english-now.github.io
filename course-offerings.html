English Now! offers various types (group, private, and semi-private) and schedules of classes to meet your needs. See below for information about Group Classes and Private/Semi-Private Lessons.

Group Classes:

  • Semi-Intensive and Intensive English Programs - This is the best option for students who want to improve their English proficiency quickly. These courses meet Monday through Thursday from 9:30 am – 2:00 pm. (For students at high beginner level or above.)
  • Conversation Courses – For students who want to improve their conversational English, with some attention to grammar. These courses meet three or four hours per week (days and times vary).
  • English for Professionals – These classes serve professionals who work in the diplomatic, health care, development, and business communities. Evening and Saturday morning classes are tailored specifically to the needs of intermediate to advanced level students who work, or want to work, in an English-language environment.
  • Youth Programs – We offer ESOL/English tutorials for younger students (at a cost far lower than most in-home tutoring) year-round. Our Summer Youth Program is also great for younger students, in June, July, or August.
  • Saturday School – Start your Saturday morning by improving your English proficiency! Choose from English for Professionals classes or private lessons, depending on your needs.
  • Group Programs – English Now! creates customized enrollment packages for groups of students (e.g., from an embassy, corporation, school, etc.), blending group classes, private lessons, or private group classes according to your needs.

Class sizes in all English Now! group courses are limited to a maximum of six students (maximum of four for Conversation classes).

Our Intensive English Program classes are offered year-round. Please see that page for a calendar of four-week terms for that program. Students may enroll in a partial term for three weeks. Students enroll in other programs for four- or eight-week terms, beginning when space is available in a class at their level of proficiency. Please contact us for more information regarding holiday schedules.

In addition to the group classes mentioned above, English Now! offers the following private and semi-private lessons:

  • Private and semi-private lessons - These lessons are structured and scheduled according to the student’s specific needs, and offer a very effective way to learn English.
  • Legal English - Private lessons for attorneys and legal practitioners with a specific focus on Legal English for professional purposes.
  • Online Programs - English Now! Online is an option for students who want to extend their classroom experience from the convenience of their home or office.
  • TOEIC Preparation – For students who intend to take the TOEIC, a test that measures English proficiency for professional workplace purposes. Study in private lessons or, possibly, semi-private lessons.
  • TOEFL Preparation – For students who intend to take the TOEFL, a test that measures English proficiency for higher education purposes. Study in private lessons or, possibly, semi-private lessons.

For more information about English Now! and our unique approach to English language study, please visit the school or contact us.
